    • Stand Alone Booking Engine App

    The Dynamic Pricing banner was originally designed to be served in a popup window within the booking process however displaying it as a static banner is perfectly acceptable.

    The stand alone booking engine can be generated in two custom styles;

    Standard Booking Engine

    Our Standard styled booking engine is perfect to fit in any space you have designated. Ideally this would be placed in a tabbed module but this can be placed anywhere on a page.

    In terms of custimisation you are able to edit the engines primary button/calender colour, border radius, font colours and box shadows

    Extended Booking Engine

    The extended booking engine would be more suitable for a larger space or a full html page. Along with the booking engine extra modules are displayed includng suppliers, a hero image, my booking section and popular usp's.

    In terms of customisation the same properties as the standard booking engine applies, primary colours can be chnaged including any background colours of the modules, you are also able to hide any of the modues you require, enabling you to have only the parts of the engine you require for you page.
